LEGAL · RISK DISCLOSURE

Risk Disclosure

Trading in leveraged products carries a high level of risk and may not be suitable for all investors. Losses could total your entire initial investment. Only trade with capital you are willing to lose.

Understanding Our Product

Prime DCX Ltd offers a web-based and mobile trading platform that allows clients to trade in leveraged financial products, including but not limited to foreign exchange (forex), contracts for difference (CFDs), and other derivative instruments. The platform serves experienced and retail traders accessing global markets.

Trading Risks

  • Market risk — prices move unpredictably and can move against your position at any time.
  • Leverage risk — leverage amplifies both gains and losses; small market moves can produce large changes in your equity.
  • Liquidity risk — under certain market conditions it may be difficult or impossible to close a position at the expected price.
  • Technical risk — connectivity issues or platform outages may prevent order placement or modification.

Your Responsibility

Clients bear responsibility for understanding the platform and the instruments they trade. Past performance is not indicative of future results. Prime DCX Ltd provides no investment, tax, or legal advice.
