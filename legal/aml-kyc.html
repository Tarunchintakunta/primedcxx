LEGAL · AML / KYC POLICY

Anti-Money Laundering & Know Your Customer Policy

Our commitment

Prime DCX Ltd is committed to the highest standards of Anti-Money Laundering (AML) compliance and Counter-Terrorism Financing (CTF). We maintain systematic controls, designate a Money Laundering Reporting Officer (MLRO), apply a risk-based approach, and run ongoing employee training programs.

What money laundering is

Money laundering is the disguising of illegally obtained proceeds to make them appear legitimate. It typically proceeds in three stages:

  • Placement — converting criminal funds into financial instruments or deposits, including “smurfing” (multiple small deposits).
  • Layering — moving funds between accounts to obscure their origin.
  • Integration — reintroducing laundered funds into circulation as apparently legitimate income.

Individual client verification

To open and operate an account, individual clients must provide:

  • Full name, date of birth, country of origin and residential address;
  • A valid government-issued photo ID (passport, driving licence or national ID card);
  • Proof of current address (utility bill or bank statement no older than 3 months);
  • Notarized English translations for documents in non-Latin characters.

Corporate client verification

Requirements vary by entity type. Subsidiaries of listed companies need minimal verification; unquoted companies must provide certificates of incorporation, articles of association, board resolutions, director identity documentation, and proof of registered address.

Activity monitoring

We operate automated and manual transaction monitoring to identify activity inconsistent with legitimate business patterns, and report suspicious activity as required by law.

Record retention

All transaction data, identification documents and AML-related files are retained for a minimum of 7 years after the account is closed.

Deposits and withdrawals

  • Funds must originate from, and return to, accounts held in the same name as the trading account;
  • No third-party transfers are permitted under applicable AML/CTF laws;
  • Withdrawals are processed first-come, first-served; refunds cannot exceed the original deposit amount per funding source.
